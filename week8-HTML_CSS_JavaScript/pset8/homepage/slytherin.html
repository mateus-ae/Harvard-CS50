About the Houses

Slytherin values ambition, leadership, self-preservation, cunning and resourcefulness. Its emblematic animal was the serpent, and its colours are emerald green and silver.

Professor Horace Slughorn is the Head of Slytherin, replacing Severus Snape, who as well replaced Slughorn as Potions Professor when he retired for the first time several years prior.

The Bloody Baron is the House ghost. The founder of the House was Salazar Slytherin.

Slytherin corresponds roughly to the element of water.

The Slytherin dormitories and common room are reached through a bare stone wall in the Dungeons. The Slytherin common room lays beneath the Black Lake. It is a long, low underground room with rough stone walls and silver lamps hanging from the ceiling.

Notorious Slytherins include Merlin, Tom Riddle, Draco Malfoy, and Dolores Umbridge.
